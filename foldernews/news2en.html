The National Bank of the Kyrgyz Republic and the People's Bank of China Discussed Expanding Cooperation

On June 4, 2025, the Chairman of the National Bank of the Kyrgyz Republic, Melis Turgunbaev, met with the Chairman of the People's Bank of China, Pan Gongsheng, in Beijing (PRC).

During the talks, the parties discussed the current state and prospects of Kyrgyz-Chinese cooperation in the financial sector. Particular attention was paid to the development of central bank digital currencies, strengthening ties between the central banks of the two countries, and expanding the use of national payment infrastructures.

The parties expressed mutual appreciation for the stable and constructive long-term cooperation, including the exchange of experience in the development and implementation of digital currencies. It was noted that such initiatives contribute to the digital transformation and sustainable development of the financial systems of both countries.

Considering the stable growth of bilateral trade turnover and expanding economic ties, practical measures to encourage settlements in national currencies were discussed. These included connecting Kyrgyz banks to China's Cross-Border Interbank Payment System (CIPS) and opening correspondent accounts in authorized banks in China.

The parties confirmed their intention to continue mutually beneficial cooperation in the key areas of activity of the central banks of both countries.
