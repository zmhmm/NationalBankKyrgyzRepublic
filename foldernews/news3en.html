Date created: 2025-05-16

On May 16, 2025, the National Bank of the Kyrgyz Republic, in honor of the International Museum Day, invites schoolchildren and students, residents and guests of Bishkek, as well as all those interested in collectible numismatic items, to an open day at the National Bank’s Numismatic Museum.

Visitors can learn about the history and development of the national currency, stages of banknote production, as well as view a unique exhibition of ancient coins found in Kyrgyzstan, including from the period of the Great Silk Road.

The museum features a collection of commemorative coins issued by the National Bank from 1995 to 2025, as well as samples of banknotes from foreign countries.

Guided tours will be held from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM.

Admission is free. Please bring an identity document (passport, student ID, driver's license).

Advance registration for tours is available by phone: 0 (312) 66-90-08; 0 (312) 61-24-14
