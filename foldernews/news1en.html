Date created: 2025-06-05

On June 5, 2025, the National Bank of the Kyrgyz Republic hosted a seminar for media representatives titled "Activities of the National Bank Amid Current Challenges" with the participation of Deputy Chairman of the National Bank, Azat Kozubekov.

In his welcoming speech, Azat Kozubekov emphasized that the National Bank places great importance on interaction and cooperation with media representatives.

"Our joint work allows us to communicate effectively with the public and deliver the most important and useful information to citizens in a timely manner. We are always open to dialogue and exchange of opinions," the Deputy Chairman of the National Bank stressed.

During the seminar, journalists were presented with presentations covering the main areas of the National Bank’s activities, as well as information on monetary policy, the state of the banking and payment systems, digitalization of banking services, protection of citizens from fraud, and more.

The event was held in the format of a discussion. Media representatives had the opportunity to ask questions of interest, which were answered in detail by representatives of the National Bank.
